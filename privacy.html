Privacy Policy

Phishguard Enterprise respects your privacy.

Information We Access

This app requests read-only access to your Gmail account using the Gmail API.

Purpose of Access

Email content is analyzed to detect phishing threats and malicious links.

Data Storage

We do NOT store, collect, or share your emails. All processing occurs locally on the device.

Data Sharing

No email content is transmitted to external servers unless required for optional security scanning services.

User Control

You may revoke Gmail access anytime via your Google Account permissions page.
